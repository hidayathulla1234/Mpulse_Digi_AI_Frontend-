Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our programs. Still have a question? Reach out via the contact form below.

Do I need a coding or technical background to join the AI courses?

+

No — most of our AI courses are built for complete beginners. The more advanced tracks like Machine Learning Engineering or MLOps do involve Python, but we teach that from the ground up if you start with our foundational courses first.

Are classes live or pre-recorded?

+

All classes are conducted live by instructors, with recordings made available afterward so you never lose a session if you miss it.

Will I get a certificate after completing an AI course?

+

Yes, every AI course includes a certificate of completion once you finish the required projects and assessments.

Which AI course should I start with?

+

If you're new to AI, start with Generative AI & Prompt Engineering or AI Tools & Productivity. If you already know Python and want a technical career path, Machine Learning Engineering or our Data Science track are better starting points.

How do I book a demo for an AI course?

+

Click "Book for Demo" on any course card. Our team will confirm a slot with you within 2 hours. There's no payment required to attend the demo.

How long is the AI-Powered Digital Marketing course?

+

The full program runs across six milestones over roughly 3 months, with 100+ hours of live sessions.

Is this course beginner-friendly?

+

Yes. The course starts from the absolute basics and builds up gradually — no prior marketing experience is required.

What kind of job or career support is included?

+

You get resume and LinkedIn revamping, portfolio reviews, mock interviews, referrals through our hiring and freelance partner network, and a full year of career mentorship after the course ends.

Will I work on real projects?

+

Yes — you'll set up real landing pages, run SEO audits, build actual ad campaigns, design email/affiliate flows, and finish with a capstone project building a complete marketing campaign for a real or simulated brand.

What payment options are available?

+

You can pay the full ₹28,000 in one shot (saving ₹2,000 off the ₹30,000 total), or pay in 2 installments of ₹15,000 each — first at enrollment and second 1 month later. Payment is made securely via Razorpay.
